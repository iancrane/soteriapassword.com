Automatic Login

You can learn all about streamlining your logins in this video.

To set up automatic logins,

  1. On the main menu, click Tools / Program Options.
  2. On the Speedups tab under Automatic Startup and Login, check Automatically log in to Soteria when the program starts.
  3. Enter the User Name and Password required to log in to your account.

Now when you launch Soteria, you don't have to log in!
