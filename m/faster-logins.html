Faster Website Logins

If you use the Auto-Copy Password feature, then when you hit Enter or click , your password is automatically copied when you go to the web site. Often, your user name is already filled-in and all you have to do is click in the web site's Password box and paste your password to log in.

Dig your Name

If your user name is not pre-filled, try clicking in the text box and hitting the down arrow on your keyboard.

The Flash

The fastest way to paste your password is to press Ctrl+V. That trick works in all of your other Windows programs too!
