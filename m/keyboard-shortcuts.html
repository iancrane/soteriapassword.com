Soteria Password™

Shortcuts & Accelerators

Soteria offers keyboard shortcuts and accelerators to support people who don't have the patience to reach for the mouse.

Shortcuts

Keyboard shortcuts use the Ctrl key in combination with other keys to perform actions.

KeysDescription
Ctrl+C Copy selected text or item
Ctrl+V Paste copied text or item
Ctrl+X Cut selected text or item

Accelerators

Accelerators use the Alt key in combination with other keys to select specific input fields.

KeysDescription
Alt+O Focus the Open field
Alt+U Focus the User field
Alt+P Focus the password field
