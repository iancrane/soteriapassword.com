Soteria Password™

Roll Your Own

You can type your own passwords into the Password box and use the Strength meter to see how strong it is. Use this option in cases where you need to be able to remember the password because you use it from a device where you do not have Soteria Password installed.

Remember that a password like Pa$$w0rd!! might be technically strong against a brute-force attack, but not if other people use it. Soteria Password doesn’t try to figure out if someone could easily guess you password; that part is up to you. Avoid simple keyboard patterns or passwords that look like common words or phrases.

Avoid reusing your passwords. Whenever possible, use Soteria Password to generate and recall passwords for you.
