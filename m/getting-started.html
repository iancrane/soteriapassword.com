Getting Started

Video: 90-Second Orientation

Click the button at the top to start entering passwords.

  • Once you have entered some passwords, just begin typing part of the Title or a word in the Notes and hit Enter to launch the website in your browser.
  • When the website loads, simply enter your user name and paste your password into the password box!
