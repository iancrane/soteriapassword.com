Maintaining Passwords

We recommend the following steps for creating or updating website passwords for your online accounts:

  1. Click Add or Edit on the toolbar.
  2. Use the Password Generator (Ctrl+G) to create a strong password.
  3. Copy the password and paste it into your browser.
  4. If the website accepts the password, save the entry in Soteria Password.
  5. Otherwise, reduce the password complexity and try again.
